Tempolar Voice Classification

We first present the results of some of our temporal voioce recognition classifiers. Below, one finds four videos of a small fragment from `Maarten van Rossem - De Podcast'. In these videos we visualise the output of our classifiers with colors. We use blue to show that the model thinks Maarten van Rossem is talking, red for Tom Jessen and green for `Neither'.

The fragment we selected gives our models a challenge, as it contains an active discussion between Maarten van Rossem and Tom Jessen. In addition, the intro-tune is still playing slightly in the background.

ResNet18 -- Testing accuracy: 98.45%

Mixture of Gaussians -- Testing accuracy: 97.30%

K Nearest Neighbors -- Testing accuracy: 96.49%

Ridge Regression -- Testing accuracy: 95.19%
